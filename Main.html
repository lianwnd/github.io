FROM YUU TO ZERO

Our second couple match, July 21, 2023

June's afternoon breeze never felt as comfortable as that one afternoon where I lay down next to you, next to us was a basket full of fruits, and we were playing a game of guessing each other's favorite fruit. I saw the way your face and eyes radiated with glee and joy when I told you that I can make you a pancake made and filled with your favorite fruit, grapes and raisins, as its filling. I plucked a piece of grape to feed to your mouth, and when you leaned towards my hand to bite it, we just expressed how much we love and feel safe around each other, without the need to let out the words “I love you.”

I love you, undeniably and deeply so. And I will let my actions speak them out for you.
